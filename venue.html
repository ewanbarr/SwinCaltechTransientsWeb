Venue & Accommodation

Venue

The venue for the meeting will be the Hameetman Auditorium in the Cahill building on the California Institute of Technology campus.

The map below shows the location of the venue in relation to Swinburne's Hawthorn Campus and the Glenferrie Train Station.

If arriving at Melbourne airport (Tullamarine), we suggest you take a taxi to the campus or your hotel. It will probably cost between $65 and $85.
